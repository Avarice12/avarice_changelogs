Date : 07/08/2022

Changelogs: 
• Bump AvariceXC to r7
• Upstream Kernel version to v4.14.290
• Upstream CAF tag to LA.UM.9.1.r1-12100.01-SMxxx0.QSSI13.0
• Compiled using Kizuna Clang 15.0 Thanks to @Nanami337
• Increase thermal trip points to 16
• Use -03 instead of -Ofast Optimization 
• Debloat debugging on qcacmn and qcacld 
• Update RCU from 5.4
• Add and Enable MGLRU
• Reconfigure RCUBoost, CpuSets, InputBoost and DevfreqBoost

• Some Scheduler Improvements
• Other fixes and improvements under the hood
